Akiki Paleontological Collection

About the Collection

APC Digital Fossil Museum is a scientific digital collection dedicated to the documentation, preservation and visualization of Lebanese fossils using modern technologies such as 3D scanning and digital archiving.

Scientific Fields

The collection focuses on paleontology, geology, fossil preservation, and reconstruction of ancient environments.

Collection Origin

The specimens are collected from geological formations of Lebanon, including marine fossiliferous layers rich in Cretaceous fossils.

Objectives

The goal of APC Collection is to create an accessible digital archive of fossils, combining scientific information with interactive 3D visualization.
